# FoundryAI Landing Page Design Prompt for UXPilot.ai

## Project Overview
**Product Name:** FoundryAI  
**Tagline:** "Forge Your Business Success with AI-Powered Planning"  
**Category:** AI SaaS / Business Planning Tool  
**Target Audience:** Entrepreneurs, startup founders, small business owners, consultants, and business strategists

---

## Brand Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Violet (#7C3AED) to Indigo (#4F46E5) gradient
- **Secondary:** Slate (#64748B) for text, white/light backgrounds
- **Accent:** Emerald (#10B981) for success states, Amber (#F59E0B) for warnings
- **Background:** White/Light gray (#F8FAFC) for light mode, Dark slate (#0F172A) for dark mode support

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter or similar)
- **Body:** Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Style:** Professional yet approachable, tech-forward but not intimidating

### Design Style
- Modern, clean, minimal UI with generous whitespace
- Rounded corners (xl/2xl) on cards and buttons
- Subtle shadows and gradients
- Glass-morphism effects for premium feel
- Dark mode support essential
- Mobile-first responsive design

## Technical Requirements

### Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile:** 320px - 639px (single column, stacked)
- **Tablet:** 640px - 1023px (2 columns where applicable)
- **Desktop:** 1024px+ (full layout)

### Animations & Interactions
- **Scroll animations:** Fade-in-up for sections
- **Hover effects:** Cards lift (translateY -4px), buttons glow
- **Micro-interactions:** Button click feedback, loading states
- **Parallax:** Subtle on hero section background

### Performance
- Lazy load images below fold
- Optimize illustrations (SVG or WebP)
- Minimize CLS with proper aspect ratios

### Accessibility
- WCAG 2.1 AA compliant
- Keyboard navigation support
- Alt text for all images
- ARIA labels on interactive elements
- Focus visible states

## Additional Notes

- Dark mode support is essential (toggle in header)
- Emphasize speed and ease-of-use throughout
- Position AI as "assistant" not replacement (human-in-the-loop)
- Show real product UI in mockups (not generic)
- Maintain consistent brand voice: Professional, empowering, innovative

**Tone:** Helpful expert who's been there. Not corporate. Not too casual. Trustworthy tech partner.
