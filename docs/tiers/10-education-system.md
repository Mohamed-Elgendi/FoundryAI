# Training & Education System

## Knowledge Accelerator

---

## Overview

The **FoundryAI Training & Education System** is a continuous learning architecture with personalized curricula, just-in-time education, and engagement mechanisms that ensure skill development from day one.

> **Core Philosophy:** *"The best education happens when you're slightly out of your depth, building something that matters, with just enough guidance to not drown."*

---

## Integration with Other Tiers

### Cross-System Learning

**Tier 1 (Foundation) Integration:**
- Brain Dump System clears mental RAM before learning sessions
- Distractions Killer ensures deep focus during study
- Emotion Controller maintains optimal learning state
- Momentum Builder tracks educational progress as life dimension

**Tier 2 (Opportunity) Integration:**
- Learning recommendations based on detected opportunities
- Skill gap analysis for selected business archetype
- Just-in-time education for Radar-identified opportunities

**Tier 3 (Build) Integration:**
- AI Build Assistant provides context-aware learning during implementation
- Error-triggered micro-learning when bugs encountered
- Documentation generation from actual build process

**Tier 4 (Personal Dev) Integration:**
- Self-Discovery alignment: Learn skills matching personality
- Mindset conditioning: Growth mindset development through challenges
- Productivity optimization: Efficient learning techniques

**Tier 6 (Monetization) Integration:**
- Revenue-stage appropriate education (don't learn scaling at $0)
- Affiliate education: How to recommend tools authentically
- Community monetization: Teaching as revenue stream
