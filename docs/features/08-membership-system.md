# Membership & Community Systems

## Tier Progression & Impact Program

---

## Overview

The **FoundryAI Membership & Community System** is a comprehensive rewards ecosystem that recognizes your journey from broke beginner to 7-figure entrepreneur. Through tiered membership levels, a loyalty points system (FoundryCoins), achievement badges, and community impact programs, we create a virtuous cycle of growth, contribution, and recognition.

> **Core Principle:** *Your progress should be celebrated, your contributions rewarded, and your success inspire others.*

## Give-Back Multiplier

### Success Obligations

As you succeed, the platform expects you to help others:

| Your Revenue | Expected Contribution | Platform Benefit |
|--------------|----------------------|------------------|
| $1K - $10K | Answer 1 question/week | Maintain community health |
| $10K - $100K | Mentor 1 beginner/month | Knowledge transfer |
| $100K+ | Sell 1 template on Marketplace | Passive income for you, proven paths for newcomers |
| $1M+ | Host monthly AMAs | Inspire next generation |

> **Note:** These are expectations, not requirements. Those who contribute earn significant rewards and recognition.
